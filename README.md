# Adventure Works Sales Analysis

## **1. Project Overview**

This project presents an analysis report of Adventure Works Company, covering data cleaning, SQL queries, and business insights related to sales, customers, products, and territories.

---

## **2. Problem Statement & Objectives**

Adventure Works specializes in designing and selling bicycles, cycling gear, and accessories, catering to cycling enthusiasts of all levels with high-quality products for performance and recreation.

The company wants to understand customer behavior, product performance, sales distribution across territories, and financial trends over three years (2020–2022).

Management needs clear answers to business questions such as:

- Who are the top customers?
- How much revenue is generated by each product and category?
- Which territories perform best?
- How many products were returned, and why?
- Which customers have the highest order value?

---

## **3. Data Collection & Description**

Data was collected from **10 datasets**, cleaned, merged, and stored for analysis.

---

## **4. Data Cleaning & Preparation**

Extensive cleaning was performed on datasets including calendar, customer, product, returns, sales, territory, and product subcategories to ensure accuracy.

<u>Steps taken for each dataset:</u>

- **Calendar** — 912 rows; extracted Year, Month, Quarter, and Day Name; removed duplicates; saved as CSV.
- **Customer** — 18,152 rows, 13 columns; changed customer key to text with prefix; handled blanks and duplicates.
- **Product** — 293 rows, 11 columns; converted key columns to text with prefixes; changed cost and price to decimal.
- **Sales** — Combined data from 2020–2022, totaling 56,049 rows; added prefixes; cleaned formatting.
- **Territory & Subcategory** — Minimal rows; cleaned for duplicates and formatting.
